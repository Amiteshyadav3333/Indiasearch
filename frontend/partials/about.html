About IndiaSearch

IndiaSearch is an AI-powered multilingual search platform built for Indian users. It combines web search, AI answers, voice interaction, image discovery, news, weather, finance, jobs, and research discovery into one accessible experience.

The goal is simple: make reliable information easier to find in the language and format people prefer. IndiaSearch is especially focused on regional access, local businesses, education, research sharing, and job discovery.
