Terms & Conditions

Welcome to IndiaSearch. By using our AI Voice Search Engine, you agree to these Terms and Conditions.

1. Usage Policy

IndiaSearch is built to promote Indian local businesses, startups, education, and job discovery. Users are prohibited from using the platform for malicious scraping, automated spam queries, or distributing harmful content.

2. AI Search Summaries

AI-generated content and search summaries are synthesized from public web sources using LLM APIs. While we target high accuracy, users should cross-reference information for critical details (e.g., official government notices or job requirements).

3. Geolocation & Local Search

By enabling location permissions, you allow IndiaSearch to customize results (such as weather, local repair shops, and business details) based on your coordinates. Location data is processed on-the-fly and never sold.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Here is how IndiaSearch handles user data.

1. Search Queries & Context

Search history and query context are saved locally in your browser's storage (`localStorage`) to provide fast history access. Logged-in sessions save history in our secure database to sync between your devices. You can clear this anytime from Settings.

2. Geolocation Access

We ask for geolocation to find repair shops (Jugaad intent), job positions, and weather details nearby. If coordinates are shared, they are rounded to 2 decimal places (approx. 1km accuracy) for cache consistency and privacy safety.

3. External API Sharing

When using AI Mode ("Ask AI") or Nutrition Scan, queries or compressed images are sent to AI API providers (Google Gemini or Groq) to generate summaries or identify food details. No personally identifiable details are sent.
